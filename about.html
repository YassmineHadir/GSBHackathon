About Needsly

This is a website for Pet lovers, made by Amal, Yassmine, Ahelee, and Evelyn. There is a survey option where new pet owners can take a survey and based on the answers they choose, it will return a list of websites/info on what necessities they will need to care for that particular pet. We mostly recommend vets' suggested websites.
